What is design thinking?

To answer this we have to agree on what design is. I performed a search on Google with the term "What is design" and the resulting findings were not surprising.

design

noun
none:design;plural:designs;
  1. a plan or drawing produced to show the look and function or workings ofa building, garment or other object before it is made.
  2. a decorative pattern.
verb
  1. decide upon the look and functioning of (a building, garment, or other object), by making a detailed drawing of it.

While I agree with the results I don't think they are the best examples. They are output focussed.

At it's heart, design is the solving of a problem.

If you're still reading, then you are at least willing to consider that design is a problem solving device, or at least can be.
